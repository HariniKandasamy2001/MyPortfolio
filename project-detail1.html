About the project

Machine hole scavenging bot is a semi automatic robot that aims at the eradication of manual scavenging without unemploying the manual scavengers and upgrading them to machine operators level. In developing countries still this illegal outlawed activity is happening, which leads to death of lakhs of manual scavengers due to poisonous gases inside the manhole.

The major reason for this may be the poor sewage structures and inability of existing solutions. Horizontal sewer line cleaning is also a major issue, thus this bot have been developed as a one stop solution for both horizontal and vertical sewer line cleaning. This bot also has it's own unique features like 5 DOF, operable in water scarce areas, mobility and portability.

My Role in the Project
